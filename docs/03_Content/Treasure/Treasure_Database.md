# Purpose

This document defines every treasure category used throughout **Yggdrasil Saga**.

It serves as the primary reference for:

- Treasure Chests
- Hidden Items
- Dungeon Rewards
- Exploration Rewards
- Rare Discoveries
- Postgame Treasure

Treasure placement should encourage exploration without making progression dependent upon hidden items.

---

# Treasure Categories

Treasure is divided into:

- Common Chests
- Hidden Treasure
- Puzzle Rewards
- Dungeon Rewards
- Boss Rewards
- Legendary Treasure
- Mythic Treasure

---

# Treasure Types

## Common Chest

Visible.

Contains:

- Gold
- Consumables
- Basic Equipment

---

## Hidden Treasure

Invisible until searched.

Contains:

- Rare Materials
- Accessories
- Collectibles

---

## Puzzle Reward

Awarded after solving environmental puzzles.

Contains:

- Equipment
- Recipes
- Rune Relics

---

## Boss Treasure

Appears after defeating story bosses.

Contains:

- Crafting Materials
- Equipment
- Gold

The Covenant Rune is **never** stored inside a treasure chest.

---

## Legendary Treasure

Hidden throughout the Nine Realms.

Contains:

- Legendary Equipment
- Rare Recipes
- Lore

---

## Mythic Treasure

Postgame only.

Found within:

- Hvergelmir
- Mythic Arena
- Secret Boss Areas

Contains the strongest rewards in the game.

# Secret Treasure

Hidden throughout every realm are:

## Rune Relics

One associated with each Covenant Rune.

Examples

- Heimdall's Horn Fragment
- Baldur's Petal
- Freyr's Seed
- Nerthus' Branch
- Thor's Stone
- Verðandi's Thread
- Surtr's Ember
- Odin's Feather
- Ullr's Arrow
- Eostre's Blossom

Collecting all Rune Relics unlocks:

- Secret Codex Entries
- Additional Ratatoskr Dialogue
- Achievement:
  **Rune Archivist**

# Treasure Respawn Rules

Normal Chests

Do not respawn.

---

Hidden Items

Do not respawn.

---

Arena Rewards

May be earned repeatedly.

---

Postgame Treasure

Unique.

Never respawns.

---

# Treasure Scaling

Treasure quality increases with realm progression.

Each new realm introduces:

- Better equipment
- Stronger crafting materials
- More gold
- More valuable accessories

---

# Canon Principles

- Covenant Runes are never found inside treasure chests.
- Story bosses award Covenant Runes directly.
- Hidden treasure rewards exploration rather than mandatory progression.
- Legendary treasure supports optional exploration.
- Mythic treasure is reserved for postgame content.
- Treasure progression mirrors overall world progression.
