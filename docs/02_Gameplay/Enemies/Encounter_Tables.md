# Purpose

This document defines all random encounter tables used throughout **Yggdrasil Saga**.

It serves as the primary reference for:

- Enemy placement
- Encounter frequency
- Region difficulty
- Experience pacing
- Material progression

Enemy statistics are defined separately in:

- Enemy_Database.md
- Enemy_Progression.md

---

# Encounter Philosophy

Every new realm should introduce:

- New enemy families
- One new combat mechanic
- New crafting materials
- Stronger equipment progression

Random encounters should reinforce the identity of each realm.

# Special Encounters

## Rare Encounters

Approximately

```text
2%
```

Chance.

Examples:

- Golden Boar
- Crystal Phoenix
- Ancient Golem
- Worldroot Spirit

Rare enemies have increased EXP and drop rates.

---

## Elite Encounters

Story-dependent.

Appear after major Rune recoveries.

Contain stronger versions of standard enemies.

Guaranteed rare material drops.

---

# Repel System

Repel prevents encounters with enemies significantly below the Hero's level.

Does not affect:

- Bosses
- Elite Encounters
- Story Battles

---

# Lure System

Enemy Lures increase encounter frequency.

Used for:

- Material farming
- Bestiary completion
- Rare enemy hunting

---

# Postgame Encounters

## Hvergelmir

Contains only:

- Mythic enemies
- Elite variants
- Ancient spirits

No standard encounters appear.

---

# Canon Principles

- Encounter difficulty follows realm progression.
- Enemy families reinforce each realm's identity.
- Rare encounters reward exploration.
- Elite encounters support postgame progression.
- Encounter tables are balanced separately from enemy statistics.
