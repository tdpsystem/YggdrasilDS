# Purpose

This document defines every major boss encounter in **Yggdrasil Saga**.

It serves as the primary reference for:

- Boss Design
- Combat Design
- Narrative Design
- Dungeon Design
- Arena Boss Rush
- Boss Rewards
- Cutscenes

Bosses should represent the central philosophy of each realm rather than acting as simple obstacles.

---

# Boss Design Philosophy

Every story boss must:

- Advance the narrative
- Test newly learned mechanics
- Reflect its realm's theme
- Feel unique
- Reward mastery rather than grinding

Each boss consists of:

- Introduction
- Multiple combat phases
- Narrative resolution
- Covenant Rune acquisition (where applicable)

# Boss Design Rules

Every boss should:

- Introduce a unique mechanic.
- Change behaviour between phases.
- Reflect the philosophy of its realm.
- Reward player learning rather than raw statistics.

Bosses should never rely solely on inflated HP values.

---

# Canon Principles

- Every realm has one primary story boss.
- Surtr is a story encounter, not a villain.
- Thrym becomes a permanent party member.
- Loki is the final narrative boss.
- Route bosses exist only after the Hero makes the final choice.
- Postgame bosses are entirely optional.
