# Purpose

This document defines the rewards obtained from every major boss encounter in **Yggdrasil Saga**.

It serves as the primary reference for:

- Story Rewards
- Covenant Rune Rewards
- Equipment Rewards
- Crafting Materials
- Experience
- Gold
- Arena Boss Rush Rewards

Boss rewards should reinforce:

- Story progression
- Realm identity
- Crafting progression
- Equipment progression

---

# Reward Structure

Every major story boss awards:

- Story Progress
- Experience
- Gold
- Covenant Rune (where applicable)
- Unique Material
- Codex Entry
- Achievement Progress

# Reward Scaling

Boss rewards should increase naturally with realm progression.

Each chapter introduces:

- Stronger crafting materials
- Better equipment
- More experience
- Higher gold rewards

---

# Canon Principles

- Every story boss grants unique crafting materials.
- Covenant Runes are story rewards, never inventory items.
- Surtr willingly grants the Rune of Fire.
- Loki's defeat awakens the Covenant Core and unlocks the final decision.
- Ending bosses award unique equipment recipes tied to their philosophy.
- Postgame bosses provide Mythic progression only.
