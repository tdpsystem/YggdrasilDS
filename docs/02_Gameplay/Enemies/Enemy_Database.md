# Purpose

This document defines every enemy family appearing throughout **Yggdrasil Saga**.

It serves as the primary reference for:

- Enemy Design
- Combat Balance
- Realm Ecology
- Drops
- Weaknesses
- Experience Values
- Bestiary

Enemy statistics are balanced separately within **Enemy_Progression.md**.

Balanced.

Few weaknesses.

---

## Mythic

Late-game enemies.

Unique mechanics.

Often immune to status effects.

# Canon Principles

- Enemy strength scales with realm progression.
- Every realm introduces new mechanics.
- Enemy drops feed directly into the Crafting System.
- Bosses are defined separately in **Boss_Design_Bible.md**.
- Standard enemies reinforce the themes of their realm.
- Mythic enemies appear only in postgame content.
