# Yggdrasil Saga

**Version:** 5.0 — Canon Edition

---

# Purpose

This document defines how enemies scale throughout **Yggdrasil Saga**.

It establishes:

- Enemy progression
- Difficulty curves
- Stat scaling
- Experience progression
- Gold progression
- Material progression
- Elite variants
- Postgame scaling

Enemy abilities are defined in **Enemy_Database.md**.

---

# Design Philosophy

Enemy progression should:

- Teach mechanics gradually
- Match realm themes
- Reward preparation
- Encourage equipment upgrades
- Support class progression

Difficulty should increase steadily without requiring excessive grinding.

# Enemy Growth

Every new realm increases:

- HP
- ATK
- DEF
- MAG
- RES
- SPD

while introducing at least one new mechanic.

---

# Elite Variants

Elite enemies appear:

- Late-game
- Arena
- Postgame
- Mythic Challenges

Modifiers

HP

×1.5

ATK

×1.3

DEF

×1.3

EXP

×2

Drops

Guaranteed rare material.

---

# Champion Variants

One champion enemy exists per realm.

Champions possess:

- Unique colour palette
- Additional skills
- Improved AI
- Exclusive drops

---

# Mythic Variants

×2.0

ATK

×1.5

MAG

×1.5

DEF

×1.5

RES

×1.5

Unique mechanics are added.

---

# Boss Scaling

Story bosses never scale.

Arena Boss Rush uses fixed statistics.

Mythic Boss Rush uses enhanced statistics.

---

# Material Progression

Enemy drops progress naturally.

Tier I

Basic crafting.

---

Tier II–IV

Equipment crafting.

---

Tier V–VII

Rare upgrades.

---

Tier VIII–X

Legendary crafting.

---

Postgame

Mythic materials.

---

# Gold Progression

Gold rewards increase steadily with realm progression.

No sudden spikes.

---

# Experience Curve

Experience rewards are designed so players remain on-level by:

- Completing the main story
- Exploring each realm
- Completing a moderate number of side quests

Grinding should never be mandatory.

---

---

# Canon Principles

- Enemy progression follows the Ten Realm journey.
- Every realm introduces new mechanics.
- Enemy scaling supports class progression.
- Story bosses remain fixed.
- Mythic scaling is reserved for postgame.
- Progression rewards preparation and strategy rather than excessive grinding.
