# Purpose

The Arena is Yggdrasil Saga's primary optional combat activity.

It provides:

- Repeatable battles
- Arena-exclusive rewards
- Equipment progression
- Achievement progression
- Boss Rush
- Endless Arena
- Mythic Challenges

The Arena exists to test mastery of the combat system rather than character level alone.

---

# Arena Philosophy

The Arena rewards:

- Strategy
- Class mastery
- Team composition
- Equipment optimisation
- Knowledge of enemy mechanics

Grinding alone should never guarantee victory.

# Canon Principles

- Arena progression is entirely optional.
- Arena Tokens are exclusive to Arena content.
- Surtr appears as a Story Trial during Boss Rush.
- Mythic Challenges unlock only after completing the game.
- Arena rewards complement—but never replace—main story progression.
