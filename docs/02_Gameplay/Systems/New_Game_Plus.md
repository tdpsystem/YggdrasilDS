# Yggdrasil Saga

## New Game Plus

**Version:** 5.0 — Canon Edition

**Authority:** CANON_REFERENCE.md

---

# Purpose

New Game Plus (NG+) allows players to begin a fresh adventure while retaining selected progression from a completed
playthrough.

It encourages experimentation with:

- Different classes
- Different equipment
- Different party builds
- Different ending routes

NG+ is designed to respect player progression while preserving the narrative structure.

---

# Unlock Requirements

Complete **any** of the three endings.

- Preservation
- Renewal
- Liberation

After the credits, New Game Plus becomes permanently available.

---

# Story Philosophy

The Hero begins another journey.

Gameplay progress is retained.

Story knowledge is not.

NPCs treat the Hero as though this is the first journey.

Only the player remembers previous playthroughs.

# Reset

The following always reset.

---

## Story

- Prologue
- Chapters
- Cutscenes

---

## Quests

- Main Quests
- Side Quests
- Companion Quests

---

## Event Flags

All story progression flags reset.

Examples:

- FLAG_PROLOGUE_STARTED
- FLAG_ASTRID_JOINED
- FLAG_RUNE_CRAFT_OBTAINED

---

## Covenant Progress

The Ten Rune Covenant resets.

Runes must be recovered again.

- Craft
- Radiance
- Light
- Growth
- Giants
- Ice
- Fire
- Wisdom
- Death
- Thunder

---

## Realm Progress

All realms become locked again.

Ratatoskr gradually restores Fast Travel as each realm is completed.

---

- Total Enemy Defeats

# Ratatoskr

Ratatoskr remains the Hero's permanent companion.

Although Fast Travel resets,

his Journal retains:

- Lore
- Codex
- Bestiary
- Exploration Records

Ratatoskr occasionally acknowledges that "this journey feels strangely familiar," but never breaks the fourth wall.

# Canon Principles

- The Hero begins a new journey.
- The world does **not** remember previous playthroughs.
- Ratatoskr remains the Hero's permanent companion.
- The Ten Rune Covenant must always be recovered again.
- All three endings are equally valid and tracked independently.
- NG+ exists to encourage mastery, experimentation, and exploration.
