# Purpose

This document defines every achievement available in **Yggdrasil Saga**.

Achievements reward:

- Story Progression
- Exploration
- Combat Mastery
- Collection
- Crafting
- Arena
- Postgame Completion

Achievements encourage players to experience every aspect of the game without requiring unnecessary grinding.

# Canon Principles

- Every ending has equal achievement value.
- Ratatoskr has his own achievement line.
- The Ten Rune Covenant replaces the old Rune achievement structure.
- Hidden achievements reward exploration and lore rather than grinding.
- 100% completion is achievable in New Game Plus.
