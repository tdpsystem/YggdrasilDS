# Purpose

This document defines every crafting mechanic used throughout **Yggdrasil Saga**.

It serves as the primary reference for:

- Equipment Crafting
- Equipment Upgrading
- Rune Forging
- Material Collection
- Crafting Progression
- Endgame Crafting

---

# Core Philosophy

Crafting should reward:

- Exploration
- Boss victories
- Resource management
- Realm progression

Crafting should never replace dungeon rewards.

Instead, it allows players to customise and improve equipment.

---

- DEF

# Rune Forging

Rune Forging becomes available after obtaining:

## Rune of Light

Rune Forging allows equipment to resonate with the Ten Rune Covenant.

# Rune Resonance

Equipment may resonate with:

- Craft
- Radiance
- Light
- Growth
- Giants
- Ice
- Fire
- Wisdom
- Death
- Thunder

Each resonance grants a passive effect.

---

## Examples

### Rune of Craft

Crafting Cost Reduced

---

### Rune of Growth

HP Regeneration

---

### Rune of Fire

Fire Damage Increased

---

### Rune of Ice

---

### Rune of Wisdom

Reduced MP Costs

---

### Rune of Thunder

Rune Skill Damage Increased

# Canon Principles

- Crafting begins in Svartalfheim.
- Thora Ironhand is the primary crafting specialist.
- Rune Forging uses Covenant Resonance rather than consuming the Ten Runes.
- The Ten Covenant Runes can never be crafted, destroyed, duplicated, or equipped.
- Mythic Crafting is reserved for postgame progression.
- Crafting complements exploration rather than replacing it.
