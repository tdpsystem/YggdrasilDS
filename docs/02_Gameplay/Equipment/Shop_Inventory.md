# Purpose

This document defines every shop available throughout **Yggdrasil Saga**.

It serves as the primary reference for:

- Item Shops
- Equipment Shops
- Crafting Vendors
- Arena Vendors
- Spirit Merchants
- Postgame Merchants

Shop inventories expand naturally as story progression advances.

- Mythic Merchant

# Pricing Philosophy

Equipment should become progressively stronger while remaining affordable through normal story progression.

Players should never need excessive grinding to purchase story-tier equipment.

---

Covenant Runes

Cannot be bought, sold, or traded.

---

# Canon Principles

- Shop inventories expand naturally through story progression.
- Crafting materials become more valuable in later realms.
- Arena Tokens are accepted only by the Arena Merchant.
- Ratatoskr provides exploration rewards rather than traditional shop services.
- Mímir's Well provides class services only.
- Covenant Runes are never purchasable items.
