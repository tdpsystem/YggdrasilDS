## Core Premise

The Ten Rune Covenant was created by the Norns at the dawn of the First Age.

The Ten Runes are not relics but cosmic seals woven into Yggdrasil itself. Each Rune anchors a fundamental force of
existence.

Nine Runes are housed within the Nine Realms.

The tenth Rune lies within the Heart of Yggdrasil.

As the Hero recovers the Runes, the worlds temporarily recover, but the Covenant weakens and the stability of Yggdrasil
declines.

The central question of the game is whether the current age should be preserved, renewed, or fundamentally changed.

## Cosmology

### The Norns

The Norns created the Ten Rune Covenant.

They do not serve Odin.

They do not serve Loki.

They preserve fate and maintain the balance of the cycle.

### Odin

Believes the current age should be preserved.

Seeks to delay Ragnarök indefinitely.

### Loki

Believes the cycle itself is a prison.

Seeks to break the cycle permanently.

### Ratatoskr

Serves neither side.

Acts as messenger between all cosmic powers.

## Endings

### Preservation Ending

Alignment:

* Odin

Outcome:

* The Ten Rune Covenant remains intact.
* Ragnarök is delayed.
* The current age survives.

Final Boss:

* Níðhöggr

---

### Renewal Ending

Alignment:

* Norns

Outcome:

* The Runes are restored.
* Ragnarök occurs.
* A new age begins.

Final Boss:

* Loki True Form

---

### Liberation Ending

Alignment:

* Loki

Outcome:

* The Covenant is destroyed.
* The cycle ends permanently.
* The future becomes unknowable.

Final Boss:

* Odin's Final Guardian
