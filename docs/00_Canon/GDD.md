# High Concept

**Yggdrasil Saga** is a turn-based JRPG developed for the Nintendo DS.

Inspired by classic titles such as **Dragon Quest IX**, it combines traditional turn-based combat with Norse mythology
and an original narrative centred on fate, memory, and choice.

Players journey across the Ten Realms to restore the **Ten Rune Covenant**, only to discover that the Covenant does not
save the world—it regulates the cycle of Ragnarök.

The player ultimately decides whether existence should be:

- Preserved
- Renewed
- Liberated

# Story Overview

The Norns awaken a new Rune Bearer beneath Yggdrasil.

Ratatoskr becomes the Hero's permanent companion.

Together they journey across the Nine Realms recovering the Ten Rune Covenant.

As more Runes are recovered the Hero discovers the truth:

The Covenant does not heal Yggdrasil.

It delays Ragnarök.

The Hero eventually stands before three competing philosophies:

- Preservation (Odin)
- Renewal (The Norns)
- Liberation (Loki)

Only one future may become reality.
