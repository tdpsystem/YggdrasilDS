# Yggdrasil Saga

## Art Bible

**Version:** 5.0 — Canon Edition

**Authority:** CANON_REFERENCE.md

---

# Purpose

This document defines the complete visual identity of **Yggdrasil Saga**.

It serves as the primary reference for:

- Environment Art
- Character Art
- Enemy Art
- Boss Art
- Portraits
- UI
- Animation
- Visual Effects

Every asset should reinforce the game's themes of mythology, memory, and the cyclical nature of existence.

---

# Art Direction

## Visual Style

Classic Nintendo DS JRPG.

Inspired by:

- Dragon Quest IX
- Final Fantasy III DS
- Golden Sun
- Norse Mythology

Visual goals:

- Bright but melancholic
- Stylised rather than realistic
- Readable at DS resolution
- Distinct silhouettes

---

# Overall Tone

The world should feel:

- Ancient
- Mythic
- Hopeful
- Beautiful
- Slowly decaying

The player should always sense that something beneath the surface is changing.

---

# Character Style

Characters should use:

- Clean outlines
- Expressive faces
- Simple silhouettes
- Bold colour palettes

Avoid excessive realism.

---

# Hero

Design Goals

- Easily recognisable
- Adaptable to every class
- Neutral appearance
- Distinct silhouette

---

# Ratatoskr

Design Goals

Friendly.

Expressive.

Energetic.

Should remain visually memorable throughout the game.

Despite being mythological,

Ratatoskr should feel approachable.

# Hel

Design Goals

Powerful.

Magic effects should emphasise:

- Runes
- Nature
- Light
- Fire
- Ice
- Thunder

| Rune     | Visual Theme        |
|----------|---------------------|
| Craft    | Orange runic sparks |
| Radiance | Golden light        |
| Light    | White glow          |
| Growth   | Green vines         |
| Giants   | Stone fragments     |
| Ice      | Frost crystals      |
| Fire     | Flame sigils        |
| Wisdom   | Blue runes          |
| Death    | Violet spirits      |
| Thunder  | White lightning     |

---

# Visual Progression

As more Runes are recovered:

- Realms appear healthier.
- Yggdrasil appears weaker.
- Roots slowly crack.
- Covenant energy becomes unstable.

The player should subconsciously notice this long before it is explained.

---

# Canon Principles

- Art should support storytelling.
- Every realm must have a unique visual identity.
- Ratatoskr should always appear welcoming.
- Loki should never visually resemble a traditional villain until the finale.
- The Ten Rune Covenant should always appear ancient and sacred.
- Visual storytelling should reinforce the slow weakening of the Covenant.
