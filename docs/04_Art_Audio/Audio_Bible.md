# Purpose

This document defines the complete audio direction of **Yggdrasil Saga**.

It serves as the primary reference for:

- Background Music
- Battle Music
- Boss Themes
- Ambient Audio
- Sound Effects
- Character Motifs
- Audio Identity

Every piece of music should reinforce the themes of memory, mythology, and the cyclical nature of existence.

---

# Audio Philosophy

Music should feel:

- Ancient
- Hopeful
- Emotional
- Melancholic
- Mythic

Silence should be used deliberately during moments of revelation.

---

# Audio Progression

As the Rune Covenant weakens:

- Realm themes become slightly more unstable.
- Ambient sounds grow quieter.
- Dissonance gradually increases.
- The Yggdrasil motif appears more frequently.

Most players should notice this subconsciously rather than consciously.

---

# Canon Principles

- Ratatoskr has his own recurring musical motif.
- Loki's music evolves from friendly to tragic.
- Surtr's theme reflects duty rather than evil.
- Every realm has a unique musical identity.
- The final ending themes reflect the philosophy chosen by the player.
- Audio should support emotional storytelling without overwhelming it.
