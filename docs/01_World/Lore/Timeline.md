# The First Age

## Ginnungagap

Before time itself, only the Great Void existed.

Between:

- Muspelheim
- Niflheim

Fire met Ice.

From their meeting, life emerged.

---

## The Birth of Ymir

The first living being.

Ancestor of all giants.

From Ymir's body the gods shaped creation.

---

## The Birth of Yggdrasil

As reality stabilized,

the World Tree grew.

Its roots reached every realm.

Its branches reached every future.

Existence became connected.

---

# The Dawn of Fate

## Arrival of the Norns

The three Norns appeared beside the roots of Yggdrasil.

- Urðr
- Verðandi
- Skuld

Rather than serving the gods,

they became guardians of Fate itself.

---

# Creation of the Ten Rune Covenant

During the First Age,

the Norns wove ten primordial Runes into Yggdrasil.

Together they became:

## The Ten Rune Covenant

Its purpose was never to prevent Ragnarök.

Instead,

it regulated the rhythm of existence.

The Covenant allowed each age enough time to flourish before reaching its natural conclusion.

---

# The Age of Gods

Asgard rose to prominence.

Odin became ruler of the gods.

The realms prospered beneath Yggdrasil.

---

## Odin's Sacrifice

Seeking ultimate knowledge,

Odin sacrificed one of his eyes.

Later,

he hung upon Yggdrasil itself,

learning the deepest mysteries of the Runes.

Even so,

he never learned who truly created the Covenant before the Norns shaped it into its present form.

---

## The Birth of Ratatoskr

Ratatoskr became messenger of the World Tree.

His duty:

Carry messages between every realm.

Carry warnings between every age.

He would continue this duty throughout every cycle.

---

# The First Ragnarök

Eventually,

the First Age reached its conclusion.

The Covenant permitted Ragnarök to unfold.

Worlds burned.

Worlds froze.

Civilizations vanished.

Yet life returned.

The cycle continued.

---

# The Forgotten Ages

Thousands of years passed.

The cycle repeated.

Again.

And again.

Entire civilizations rose and disappeared.

Every age believed itself unique.

None realized countless worlds had already existed before them.

---

# The Rune Bearers

Whenever the Covenant weakened,

a Rune Bearer emerged.

Each gathered the Ten Runes.

Each reached the Covenant Core.

Each stood before the same choice.

None possessed the complete truth.

Every one chose Preservation.

The cycle continued.

---

# The Lost Rune Wars

One forgotten age discovered fragments of Rune power.

Kingdoms fought for control.

The wars devastated multiple realms.

Entire cultures disappeared.

After Ragnarök,

only scattered memories remained.

---

# The Age of Giants

In one previous cycle,

the giants dominated several realms.

Rather than fearing Ragnarök,

they accepted it.

Fragments of their philosophy survived into the current age through giant oral tradition.

---

# The Previous Age

## The Last Hero

The Rune Bearer of the previous age recovered all Ten Runes.

They discovered that the Covenant regulated Ragnarök.

Without understanding the complete consequences,

they chose Preservation.

Their memories became part of Helheim.

Their echo became:

## Memory of the Last Hero

Guardian of the Rune of Death.

---

# The Current Age

The age in which Yggdrasil Saga takes place.

---

## Rise of the Nine Realms

Civilization flourishes once more.

- Midgard prospers.
- Alfheim shines.
- Svartalfheim forges wonders.
- Vanaheim blooms.
- Jötunheim endures.
- Niflheim preserves forgotten memories.
- Muspelheim prepares for its destined role.
- Asgard watches.
- Helheim remembers.

---

## Ratatoskr's Journey

As the Covenant weakens,

Ratatoskr resumes travelling between the realms.

He quietly notices signs that the current age resembles countless previous ones.

He searches for someone capable of making a different choice.

---

## Loki Remembers

Unlike every other being,

Loki remembers every previous cycle.

Every civilization.

Every victory.

Every tragedy.

He grows convinced the cycle itself has become a prison.

Rather than preserving or renewing it,

he begins searching for a way to destroy it forever.

---

## Odin Waits

Odin also knows the truth.

Unlike Loki,

he believes every generation deserves more time.

He prepares Asgard for the coming crisis.

---

# Year Zero

## Awakening Beneath Yggdrasil

The Norns awaken a new Rune Bearer.

Ratatoskr becomes their companion.

The Hero begins searching for the Covenant Runes.

Believing:

> They will heal Yggdrasil.

# The Three Philosophies

## Preservation

Representative:

Odin

Belief:

Protect the present age.

---

## Renewal

Representatives:

The Norns

Belief:

Allow the cycle to continue.

---

## Liberation

Representative:

Loki

Belief:

Destroy the Covenant.

End the cycle forever.

# Canon Principles

- The Norns created the Ten Rune Covenant.
- Ratatoskr has served every age as messenger.
- Loki remembers every previous cycle.
- Every previous Rune Bearer chose Preservation.
- The Hero is the first Rune Bearer to know the complete truth before making the final decision.
- The future of existence has never truly been free—until the events of Yggdrasil Saga.
