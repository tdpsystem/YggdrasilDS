# The Ten Rune Covenant

The Ten Rune Covenant was created by the Norns during the First Age.

Contrary to popular belief, the Covenant was **never created to prevent Ragnarök**.

Instead, it regulates the rhythm of existence itself.

Each Rune anchors one fundamental force of reality.

Together they stabilize:

- Fate
- Time
- Life
- Death
- Memory
- Growth
- Civilization
- The World Tree itself

---

# Why the Covenant Exists

Without the Covenant:

- Reality would become unstable.
- The Nine Realms would drift apart.
- Ragnarök would occur uncontrollably.

The Covenant slows the cycle long enough for each age to flourish.

---

# The Hidden Truth

Throughout most of the game, everyone believes:

> "Recover the Runes to heal Yggdrasil."

This is only partially true.

The truth is:

> The worlds recover because the Rune returns to its proper place.

At the same time,

Removing the Rune from its guardian weakens the Covenant.

The Hero unknowingly accelerates the coming of Ragnarök.

---

# Rune Resonance

Every Rune resonates with every other Rune.

Removing one Rune weakens all remaining Runes.

This produces:

- Increasing monster activity
- Realm instability
- Environmental changes
- Declining Tree Stability
- Stronger manifestations of primordial forces

---

# The Covenant Core

At the Heart of Yggdrasil rests the Covenant Core.

The Core binds all Ten Runes together.

It regulates:

- Realm Stability
- Fate
- Rune Resonance
- The Cycle of Ragnarök

Only after recovering all Ten Runes can the Core awaken.

# Previous Rune Bearers

Throughout history,

individuals known as Rune Bearers arose whenever the Covenant weakened.

Every Rune Bearer recovered all Ten Runes.

Every Rune Bearer reached the Covenant Core.

Every Rune Bearer ultimately chose Preservation.

The Memory of the Last Hero is the most recent Rune Bearer before the current Hero.

---

# Rune Fragments

The power used by mortals is not drawn directly from the Runes.

Instead,

Rune Magic comes from tiny fragments resonating with the Covenant.

This prevents mortals from wielding the full power of a World Rune.

---

# Rune Magic

Rune Magic developed independently in every age.

Although cultures differ,

every magical tradition ultimately traces its origin to the Ten Rune Covenant.

---

# Final Revelation

The Hero eventually discovers:

The Covenant was never designed to save the world forever.

It exists so that every age may choose its own future.

The Hero becomes the first Rune Bearer capable of making that decision with complete knowledge.

---

# Canon Principles

- The Ten Rune Covenant was created by the Norns.
- Every Rune anchors a fundamental force of existence.
- Recovering a Rune restores its realm while weakening the Covenant.
- The Rune of Thunder is the final World Rune recovered within the Heart of Yggdrasil.
- The Hero is the first Rune Bearer to make an informed choice.
- The future of existence ultimately belongs to the Hero alone.
