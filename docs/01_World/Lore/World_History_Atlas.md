# Historical Eras

The known history of existence is divided into eight great eras.

1. The Age of Creation
2. The Age of Fate
3. The Age of the Covenant
4. The Age of Gods
5. The Forgotten Ages
6. The Age of the Last Hero
7. The Current Age
8. The Age of Choice

---

# The Age of Creation

## Ginnungagap

Before existence there was only the Great Void.

Between:

- Muspelheim
- Niflheim

Fire met Ice.

Life emerged.

---

## The Birth of Ymir

The first giant.

Ancestor of giantkind.

His body became the foundation of the world.

---

## The Birth of Yggdrasil

As reality stabilized,

the World Tree emerged.

Its roots reached every realm.

Its branches stretched beyond time.

Yggdrasil became the foundation upon which existence itself depended.

---

# The Age of Fate

## The Arrival of the Norns

The Three Norns appeared beside the roots of Yggdrasil.

- Urðr
- Verðandi
- Skuld

They became guardians of Fate.

They served neither Odin nor Loki.

Their responsibility became maintaining the balance of existence.

---

## The Birth of Ratatoskr

Ratatoskr became messenger of the World Tree.

His duties:

- Carry messages
- Observe every age
- Witness every cycle

Unlike Loki,

Ratatoskr gradually forgot much of what came before.

Only fragments remain.

---

# The Age of the Covenant

## Creation of the Ten Rune Covenant

The Norns forged ten primordial Runes into Yggdrasil.

Together they became:

## The Ten Rune Covenant

The Covenant regulated:

- Fate
- Time
- Life
- Death
- Memory
- Ragnarök

It was never intended to prevent endings.

Only to delay them.

---

## The Covenant Core

Deep beneath Yggdrasil,

the Covenant Core bound all Ten Runes together.

Few beings ever learned it existed.

---

# The Age of Gods

Asgard rose.

The gods established their kingdoms.

The Nine Realms flourished beneath the World Tree.

---

## Odin's Sacrifice

Seeking wisdom,

Odin surrendered his eye.

Later,

he hung upon Yggdrasil,

learning the mysteries of Rune magic.

Even Odin never learned everything.

Some truths remained known only to the Norns.

---

## The Divine Guardians

Throughout history,

certain gods became guardians of individual Covenant Runes.

They did not own them.

They protected them.

---

# The Forgotten Ages

The cycle repeated countless times.

Every age believed itself unique.

Every age eventually ended.

Civilizations rose.

Civilizations vanished.

Only fragments survived.

---

## The Lost Rune Wars

One forgotten civilization discovered Rune fragments.

Nations fought over their power.

The resulting wars devastated several realms.

Almost every record was lost during the next Ragnarök.

---

## The Giant Kingdoms

One age saw giantkind dominate much of existence.

Unlike most civilizations,

they accepted Ragnarök.

Their philosophy survived through oral tradition.

---

# The Age of the Last Hero

The Rune Bearer before the current Hero recovered every Rune.

They reached the Covenant Core.

They learned part of the truth.

Believing Preservation to be the only responsible choice,

they continued the cycle.

Their spirit remained behind.

It became:

## Memory of the Last Hero

Guardian of the Rune of Death.

---

# The Weakening Covenant

The Ten Rune Covenant slowly loses stability.

Signs appear across every realm.

Examples:

- Strange weather
- Fading light
- Increased monsters
- Weakening roots
- Ancient ruins awakening

Ratatoskr notices these signs long before anyone else.

---

# Loki's Decision

Unlike every other being,

Loki remembers every previous cycle.

Eventually,

he concludes that endless repetition is no longer mercy.

It is imprisonment.

He decides to seek Liberation.

---

# The Hero Awakens

The Norns awaken a new Rune Bearer.

Ratatoskr becomes their companion.

The search for the Ten Runes begins.

---

# The Age of Choice

For the first time since the First Age,

the Hero reaches the Covenant Core knowing the complete truth.

Three philosophies stand before them.

---

## Preservation

Representative:

Odin

Belief:

Protect the present.

---

## Renewal

Representatives:

The Norns

Belief:

Continue the cycle.

---

## Liberation

Representative:

Loki

Belief:

Destroy the Covenant.

# Core Historical Principle

> Every age believes it is the first.
>
> Every age believes it will be the last.
>
> The Hero becomes the first to understand they are neither.

---

# Canon Principles

- The Ten Rune Covenant was created by the Norns.
- Ratatoskr has witnessed every age.
- Loki remembers every cycle.
- Every previous Rune Bearer chose Preservation.
- The current Hero becomes the first Rune Bearer capable of making a truly informed choice.
- The Age of Choice is unique in the history of existence.
