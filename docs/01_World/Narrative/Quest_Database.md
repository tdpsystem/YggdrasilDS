# Canon Notes

- Ratatoskr is the Hero's permanent companion.
- The Norns begin the Hero's journey.
- Loki is introduced as a mysterious traveller.
- The Rune order follows the Ten Rune Covenant.
- The World Rune (Thunder) is recovered inside the Heart of Yggdrasil.
- Three ending routes are available:
    - Preservation
    - Renewal
    - Liberation
