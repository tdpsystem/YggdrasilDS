# Purpose

This document provides the narrative structure for every major cinematic sequence in **Yggdrasil Saga**.

Unlike **Cutscene_Event_List.md**, which defines triggers and flags, this document defines:

- Cinematic flow
- Camera direction
- Character movement
- Dialogue pacing
- Music cues
- Emotional beats

---


## CUT_0000

# Awakening Beneath Yggdrasil

### Location

Roots of Yggdrasil

---

### Music

**The First Roots**

Slow piano.

Soft choir.

Wind ambience.

---

### Camera

Fade from black.

Slow pan across enormous roots.

Ancient runes glow beneath the bark.

The Hero lies unconscious.

---

### Scene

The Hero awakens.

Silence.

A gentle voice echoes.

---

### Urðr

> "Another age draws to its end."

---

### Verðandi

> "The Covenant weakens."

---

### Skuld

> "A choice approaches."

---

The Hero stands.

The roots tremble.

Ratatoskr climbs down from the branches above.

---

### Ratatoskr

> "Well... you're awake."

> "Good."

> "Things have become rather complicated."

---

Camera widens.

Yggdrasil fills the sky.

Fade.

---

Gameplay Begins.

# Canon Principles

- Ratatoskr is the emotional heart of the story.
- Loki is introduced as a mysterious traveller.
- The Norns never force the Hero's choice.
- Odin, the Norns and Loki each sincerely believe they are protecting the future.
- The Hero always makes the final decision alone.
