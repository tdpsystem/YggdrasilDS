## Canon Principles

- Every realm temporarily recovers after its Rune is reclaimed.
- Every Rune reclaimed weakens the Ten Rune Covenant.
- Ratatoskr remains neutral throughout the story.
- Odin represents Preservation.
- The Norns represent Renewal.
- Loki represents Liberation.
- The Hero alone determines the fate of the current age.
