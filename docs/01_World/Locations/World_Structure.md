# Overview

Yggdrasil connects every realm through its roots, trunk, and branches.

The Hero journeys across the Nine Realms before returning to the Heart of Yggdrasil itself.

Throughout the adventure the Hero recovers the **Ten Rune Covenant**, ancient cosmic seals that regulate the cycle of
Ragnarök.

Although each recovered Rune appears to restore its realm, every recovered Rune also weakens the Covenant, bringing the
current age closer to its end.

The central question of the game is:

> **Should the current age be preserved, renewed, or should the cycle itself be broken?**

---

# Structure of Yggdrasil

The World Tree is divided into four major regions.

```text
                Upper Branches
                     │
                 ┌── Asgard
                 └── Alfheim

                     │

                 Great Trunk
                     │
      Midgard ─ Svartalfheim ─ Vanaheim

                     │

                Deep Roots
                     │
      Jötunheim ─ Niflheim ─ Muspelheim

                     │

                  Helheim

                     │

            Heart of Yggdrasil
```

Each realm contains:

- One major settlement
- One overworld region
- One primary dungeon
- One Covenant Rune
- One major story boss
- One major narrative revelation

- Guardian: Verðandi

### Story Boss

Primordial Wyrm

### Party Member

None

### Narrative Purpose

Within the Frozen Archive, memories of previous ages remain perfectly preserved.

The Hero witnesses countless previous cycles of Ragnarök.

Entire civilizations have risen and vanished.

The Memory Archives reveal:

- The Rune Covenant has existed since the First Age.
- Previous Rune Bearers reached the same decision.
- The current age is only one of many.

For the first time, Loki admits he remembers previous cycles.

Ratatoskr quietly reveals that he has carried messages between every age, though even he does not remember every detail.

Loki

### Narrative Purpose

Deep within the Heart of Yggdrasil, the Hero discovers the Covenant Core.

The Hero finally understands:

- The Ten Rune Covenant was created by the Norns.
- The Covenant regulates the cycle of Ragnarök.
- Every previous Rune Bearer ultimately chose to preserve the cycle.

- The Norns wish to preserve the cycle of renewal.
- Loki wishes to destroy the Covenant itself.

Ratatoskr remains neutral.

He has served every age and every choice.

For the first time in existence, the Hero is free to decide the fate of the Nine Worlds.

# Canon Principles

- Every realm represents one fundamental aspect of existence.
- Every Rune recovered weakens the Rune Covenant.
- Ratatoskr remains neutral throughout the conflict.
- Odin represents Preservation.
- The Norns represent Renewal.
- Loki represents Liberation.
- The Hero alone decides which future becomes reality.
