# Faction_Database.md

## Yggdrasil Saga

Version: 2.0 — Canon Aligned

# Purpose

Defines the major factions operating across the Ten Realms, including their goals, leaders, relationships, territories,
and narrative roles.

---

# Core Principle

```text
Every faction believes it is protecting the future.
```

No major faction is purely evil.

Conflict comes from different beliefs about fate, Ragnarök, preservation, renewal, and freedom.

# The Rune Covenant

## Classification

Ancient System / Metaphysical Faction

## Headquarters

Heart of Yggdrasil

## Alignment

Fate / Cycle Control

## Leader

Covenant Core

## Purpose

Regulate the cycle of Ragnarök through the Ten Runes.

## Known Agents

* Covenant Core
* Rune Guardians
* Ancient mechanisms
* Echoes of previous cycles

## Story Role

The hidden force behind the entire conflict.

At first, the Runes appear to be tools of healing.

Later, the Hero learns they are seals connected to the Covenant.

## Route Role

* Preservation: Covenant remains intact
* Renewal: Covenant continues the cycle
* Liberation: Covenant Core is destroyed

---

# Odin's Preservationists

## Classification

Major Faction

## Headquarters

Asgard

## Alignment

Preservation

## Leader

Odin

## Purpose

Preserve the current age and delay Ragnarök.

## Belief

The present world is flawed, but alive.

Protecting it is worth the cost of delaying the cycle.

## Known Members

* Odin
* Most Einherjar
* Loyal Asgardian commanders
* Rune scholars of Asgard

## Player Relationship

Initially cautious.

Becomes a possible ally during the final choice.

## Story Role

Represents the Preservation route.

## Route Role

Primary allied faction in the Preservation ending.

# The Norns

## Classification

Ancient Order

## Headquarters

Yggdrasil Sanctuary

## Alignment

Renewal

## Leaders

* Urd
* Verdandi
* Skuld

## Purpose

Maintain the natural cycle of endings and rebirth.

## Belief

All things must end so new life can begin.

Ragnarök is not failure.

It is renewal.

## Known Members

* Urd
* Verdandi
* Skuld
* Norn attendants

* Root guardians

## Story Role

Represent the Renewal route.

## Route Role

Primary allied faction in the Renewal ending.

# Loki's Liberators

## Classification

Major Faction

## Headquarters

None fixed

## Alignment

Liberation

## Leader

Loki

## Purpose

Break the Rune Covenant and free existence from the cycle.

## Belief

No world should be bound forever by fate.

An unknown future is better than endless repetition.

## Known Members

* Loki
* Hidden sympathizers
* Certain giants
* Certain spirits
* Cycle-aware outcasts

## Player Relationship

Loki appears as a recurring mentor.

His true goal is revealed gradually.

## Story Role

Represents the Liberation route.

## Route Role

Primary allied faction in the Liberation ending.

# Ratatoskr's Messengers

## Classification

Neutral Network

## Headquarters

Yggdrasil

## Alignment

Neutral / Communication

## Leader

None

## Key Member

Ratatoskr

## Purpose

Carry messages between the branches, trunk, and roots of Yggdrasil.

## Belief

Knowledge must travel, even when truth is dangerous.

## Known Members

* Ratatoskr
* Lesser messengers
* Tree-path scouts

## Player Relationship

Ratatoskr is the Hero's primary guide, companion, travel system, journal keeper, and bestiary keeper.

## Story Role

Connects realms and factions without fully belonging to any one ideology.

# Endgame Alignment

## Preservation Route

### Representative

Odin

### Primary Allies

* Odin's Preservationists
* Most Einherjar
* Some mortal settlements

### Primary Enemy

* Níðhöggr

### Result

The Covenant remains intact.

Ragnarök is delayed.

---

## Renewal Route

### Representative

The Norns

### Primary Allies

* The Norns
* Verdant Circle
* Hel's Silent Court
* Some spirits and giants

### Primary Enemy

* Loki

### Result

Ragnarök proceeds.

The world ends and is reborn.

The cycle continues.

---

## Liberation Route

### Representative

Loki

### Primary Allies

* Loki's Liberators
* Some giants
* Some cycle-aware spirits
* Those who reject fate

### Primary Enemy

* Covenant Core

### Result

The Rune Covenant is destroyed.

The cycle ends permanently.

The future becomes unknown.
