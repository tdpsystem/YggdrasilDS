# Permanent Companion

## Ratatoskr

Ratatoskr accompanies the Hero throughout the entire game.

Unlike party members, Ratatoskr never participates in battle.

Functions:

- Fast Travel
- Journal
- Lore Commentary
- Messenger between Cosmic Powers
- Quest Guidance
- Optional Conversations

Ratatoskr remains completely neutral throughout the conflict between Odin, the Norns and Loki.

# Hero

## Role

Rune Bearer

Chosen by the Norns.

---

## Biography

The Hero awakens beneath the roots of Yggdrasil with no memory of their past.

Chosen by the Norns, they become the newest Rune Bearer entrusted with restoring the Ten Rune Covenant.

Throughout the journey they slowly discover that every Rune recovered weakens the Covenant.

Eventually the Hero becomes the first Rune Bearer to learn the complete truth before making the final decision.

---

## Personality

The Hero is largely player-defined.

Dialogue choices should remain:

- Calm
- Compassionate
- Curious
- Determined

---

# Astrid

## Role

First Party Member

---

## Biography

Astrid is a shieldmaiden from Mannheim Village.

She joins the Hero after the defeat of the Draugr Jarl.

Although she initially believes recovering the Covenant Runes is unquestionably right, she gradually learns to question
long-held beliefs while never abandoning her loyalty to her friends.

---

## Personality

- Brave
- Honest
- Loyal
- Protective

---

## Combat Role

Frontline defender.

Excels at:

- Protection
- Counterattacks
- Shield techniques

---

## Story Purpose

Represents hope and unwavering loyalty.

---

# Eirik

## Role

Scholar

Explorer

Historian

---

## Biography

Eirik is an explorer from Alfheim.

His fascination with forgotten history leads him to accompany the Hero after the recovery of the Rune of Radiance.

He becomes increasingly obsessed with uncovering the true origin of the Rune Covenant.

---

## Personality

- Curious
- Clever
- Patient
- Observant

---

## Combat Role

Support and ranged specialist.

Excels at:

- Enemy analysis
- Buffs
- Precision attacks

---

## Story Purpose

Represents curiosity and the pursuit of truth.

---

# Thora Ironhand

## Role

Master Runesmith

---

## Biography

Thora Ironhand is one of Svartalfheim's greatest craftswomen.

She discovers ancient forge records suggesting that the Rune Covenant predates even the gods.

Determined to uncover the truth, she joins the Hero.

---

## Personality

- Practical
- Intelligent
- Blunt
- Reliable

---

## Combat Role

Heavy offensive support.

Excels at:

- Weapon enhancement
- Rune forging
- Defensive buffs

---

## Story Purpose

Represents creation and innovation.

---

# Thrym

## Role

Giant Chieftain

---

## Biography

Thrym rules one of the greatest giant clans of Jötunheim.

Unlike many mortals, he does not fear Ragnarök.

He believes endings are simply another stage of existence.

After testing the Hero's strength, he joins the party to witness whether fate itself can truly be changed.

---

## Personality

- Wise
- Calm
- Honorable
- Direct

---

## Combat Role

Heavy physical attacker.

Excels at:

- Massive damage
- Crowd control
- High durability

---

## Story Purpose

Represents strength tempered by wisdom.

# Ratatoskr

## Role

Permanent Companion

Messenger of Yggdrasil

---

## Biography

Ratatoskr has carried messages between the branches and roots of Yggdrasil since the First Age.

Although he has witnessed countless cycles, much of his own memory has faded with time.

He remembers enough to recognize patterns—but never enough to decide the Hero's path.

---

## Personality

- Curious
- Cheerful
- Wise
- Compassionate

---

## Story Purpose

Represents neutrality.

Ratatoskr never tells the Hero what to choose.

# Loki

## Role

Mysterious Traveller

Representative of Liberation.

---

## Biography

Loki appears throughout the Hero's journey as an enigmatic traveller.

Helpful yet strangely knowledgeable, he quietly guides the Hero toward difficult questions rather than easy answers.

Unlike every other being, Loki remembers every previous cycle of existence.

The endless repetition has become unbearable.

He seeks not to destroy the world—

but to destroy the cycle itself.

---

## Personality

- Charming
- Intelligent
- Witty
- Manipulative
- Deeply weary

---

## Story Purpose

Represents Liberation.

---

# Hel

## Role

Ruler of Helheim

---

## Biography

Hel governs the realm of the dead.

She neither opposes nor supports Ragnarök.

Instead she ensures that every ending is treated with dignity.

She understands Loki's suffering better than anyone else.

---

## Personality

- Quiet
- Compassionate
- Detached
- Honest

---

## Story Purpose

Represents acceptance.

---

# Surtr

## Role

Guardian of the Rune of Fire

---

## Biography

Surtr understands that his role is not conquest.

He exists to ensure the cycle continues when its time arrives.

He willingly entrusts the Rune of Fire to the Hero after testing their resolve.

---

## Personality

- Proud
- Direct
- Unyielding

---

## Story Purpose

Represents transformation.

# Canon Principles

- Ratatoskr is a permanent companion.
- The Hero is the only Rune Bearer of the current age.
- Odin represents Preservation.
- The Norns represent Renewal.
- Loki represents Liberation.
- Every major character believes they are protecting the future.
- No major character considers themselves the villain.
